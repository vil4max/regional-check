Regional Check Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Regional Check (“the App”) is a minimal CarPlay and iPhone utility by Maksim Vilchevskiy.

What we collect

The App uses your device location only while the App is in use, to determine which region to show. Location is processed on device and is not uploaded to our servers. We do not create accounts, and we do not collect name, email, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.

Third-party data

When you open or refresh, the App requests a public regional notice status from a third-party data source over the network. That request is needed to display Quiet or Loud for your region. We do not sell data and we do not use analytics SDKs or ads.

Data sharing and tracking

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data for tracking across apps or websites. We do not use your data for advertising.

Retention

The App may keep the last selected region on device for convenience. Clearing the App or reinstalling removes local data.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
